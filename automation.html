Positive Impacts of Automation

Advancements in computing have allowed for robots to be created with the functional to automate menial tasks. These robots can perform functions like vacuuming and lawnmowing and serve to help convenience people. Automation of these tasks can save lots of time, allowing owners of such robots to spend more time doing things that they enjoy

Automation uses many different technologies in order to perform their jobs, some are listed below:

  • Computer Vision
  • Embedded Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence
